Parking Policy for Private Jets

1. Reservation and Booking:

All private jet parking must be reserved in advance.

Customers can book parking spaces through the designated reservation system or by contacting the parking management.

2. Parking Duration:

The parking duration is calculated based on the number of hours or days the private jet occupies the parking space.

Customers are responsible for ensuring that the parking duration is accurately specified during the reservation.

Parking Policy for Private Jets Reservation and Booking: All private jet parking must be reserved in advance. Customers can book parking spaces through the designated reservation system or by contacting the parking management. Parking Duration: The parking duration is calculated based on the number of hours or days the private jet occupies the parking space. Customers are responsible for ensuring that the parking duration is accurately specified during the reservation.

3. Parking Fees:

Parking fees are determined based on the parking duration and the type of private jet. The fee structure may vary depending on the airport or parking facility. Customers will be informed of the applicable parking fees at the time of reservation. Cancellations and Changes: Customers should notify the parking management in advance for any cancellations or changes to their parking reservation. Depending on the parking facility's policy, there may be cancellation or rescheduling fees. Access to Parking Area: Only authorized personnel and individuals with valid security clearances are allowed access to the private jet parking area. Customers should follow the instructions provided by the parking management for access and security procedures.

Compliance with Regulations: Private jet owners and operators are responsible for complying with all local, national, and international regulations regarding parking, security, and safety. Maintenance and Fueling: Private jet owners and operators are responsible for maintenance and fueling of their aircraft. Parking facilities may provide additional services such as fueling arrangements or maintenance assistance upon request.

4. Security:

The parking facility will have security measures in place to protect private jets parked on-site. However, private jet owners and operators are advised to take appropriate security measures and secure their aircraft and belongings.

5. Liability:

The parking facility will take reasonable precautions to ensure the security and safety of parked private jets. However, the parking facility will not be held liable for any damages, theft, or loss that may occur to the private jet or its contents.

6. Compliance with Instructions:

Customers must comply with all instructions provided by the parking management regarding parking procedures, safety protocols, and any other relevant guidelines.
